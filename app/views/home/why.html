why bettermeans?

The way we work together, needs an upgrade

We face complex and interconnected social, environmental and economic problems. A lot of us want to work toward solving them. We are learning that these problems are interconnected, and complex.

We are trying to solve these problems using organizational structures that were invented before the light bulb! Industrial-age orgs are not smart or flexible enough to navigate the challenges we are facing.

We need a new agreement of how we work together. How we make decisions. How we decide on who gets to work on what. And who gets paid what.
The significant problems we have cannot be solved at the same level of thinking with which we created themAlbert Einstein
