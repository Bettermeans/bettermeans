IRB-Preferred

We'll help you understand common IRB concerns

Because web-based psychological research is relatively recent and IRBs are still in the process of developing policies and procedures to manage these new tools, it is the researcher's job to help inform IRB members about online research.

We provide you with information on informed consent, online security, handling identifying information, to help you inform your research application as it relates to your IRB about online research.
