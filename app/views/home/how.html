how we can work differently

This is our story.

we face complex and interconnected social problems. a lot of us want to work toward solving them. we are learning that these problems are interconnected, and complex. we are also learning that they have to be solved from a mindset different than the mindset that created it

The significant problems we have cannot be solved at the same level of thinking with which we created them
